The GSA at Arvada West High School works to create a welcoming environment for all people, regardless of sexual orientation or gender identity through education, support, social action, and advocacy.


We believe that schools can be truly safe ONLY when every student is assured of access to an education without fear of harassment or violence. Our Gay-Straight Alliance brings together LGBTQI+ and straight allies. All are welcome.
